Editing instructions and guidelines

To begin Adding, updating, or removing data from App Center you will enter the Admin pages by clicking the Admin link at the top right of your screen.

Once there you will see a section header labeled APP. Under that are all the data tables used to dynamically build the web page. These tables are where you will maintain all the entries. From these tables most of your work will take place within the Applications table. From there you can link to each of the other tables if the desired entry exists or even add it if it doesn't exist.

Let's start with a simple add. Click the ADD APPLICATION button at the top right of the screen. This opens a blank application form where you will fill out all the information about the application. When you get to Organization, if the organization for your application does not exist in the dropdown simply click the + button next to the dropdown, fill out the information for the new Organization, and click save. This is true for all fields except Serviceareas and Deployment environment: (if you believe you need to add to one of these please contact Francisco or Billy)

App Center will do the best that it can to display the image you choose to upload for the application, however every image is different, so if you try one and don't enjoy how it displays, simple edit the application and upload a new one. If you need help resizing an image for the best display feel free to reach out to one of us.

When you have added all the application information click save and your new application will be added to the live site. Immediately check and confirm that it is as you intended it to be, it's always good to have a second person read through to help avoid any typos or mis-formatting.
